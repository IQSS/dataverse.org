Harvard Dataverse Policies
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

These policies, terms of use and guidelines were developed for the Harvard
Dataverse with legal guidance from the `Cyberlaw Clinic <http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/teaching/cyberlawclinic>`_ at the
`Berkman Center for Internet & Society <http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/>`_ at Harvard University. 

Other Dataverse installations are free to use these as templates for setting
up their own policies, terms of use and guidelines.
